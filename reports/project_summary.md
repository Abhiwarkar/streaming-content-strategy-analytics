# Streaming Content Strategy Analytics

## Project Objective
Analyze user behavior and content performance on a streaming platform to identify trends and provide business recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python (Data Cleaning & EDA)
- SQL (Business Queries)
- Power BI (Dashboard Visualization)

## Key Insights
- Movies contribute nearly 68% of total watch time.
- International and Drama genres drive the highest engagement.
- Australia and Canada show the highest user watch time.
- Mobile and TV devices dominate platform usage.

## Business Recommendations
- Invest more in movie content acquisition.
- Promote top performing genres.
- Target high engagement countries for marketing campaigns.
- Optimize streaming experience on mobile and TV devices.
